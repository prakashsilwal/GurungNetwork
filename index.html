About Us

At DNET DIGITAL NETWORKING TECHNOLOGY, we specialize in delivering complete networking solutions for residential, commercial, and institutional spaces. With a focus on quality, performance, and long-term reliability, we help you stay connected with confidence.

  • Structured Cabling & Infrastructure

    We plan and install high-performance cabling systems for buildings, schools, and offices—ensuring fast, stable, and scalable connectivity.

  • Network Setup & Configuration

    From internet setup to switch and router configuration, we create secure and efficient networks tailored to your unique needs.

  • Maintenance & Ongoing Support

    Our job doesn’t end after installation. We provide regular maintenance, upgrades, and troubleshooting to keep your network running at peak performance.

Services

Trust us to deliver reliable, secure, and expertly crafted networking and IT solutions—tailored to your space and built to last.

Structured Cabling (Cat5, Cat6, Fiber, Coax)

We design and install structured cabling systems using Cat5, Cat6, fiber optics, and coaxial cables for reliable, high-speed connectivity across homes, offices, and commercial buildings.

CCTV System Setup (Axis, Lorex & More)

We install and configure high-definition CCTV systems using trusted brands like Axis and Lorex to enhance your property’s security and remote monitoring capabilities.

IDF/MDF Rack Setup

We organize and install Intermediate and Main Distribution Frame network racks with proper labeling, patch panels, and power management for clean, scalable infrastructure.

Network Security & Protection

We offer firewall configuration, access controls, and intrusion prevention to safeguard your digital assets from threats.

Maintenance & Troubleshooting

From diagnostics to upgrades, our team provides reliable, ongoing support to keep your network performing at its best.
